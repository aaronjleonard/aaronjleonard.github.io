I'm a fourth year PhD student in the [Kenneth C. Griffin Department of Economics](https://economics.uchicago.edu/) at UChicago.

My interests are in development economics, industrial organization and behavioral economics.

My CV is available [here](https://github.com/aaronjleonard/aaronjleonard.github.io/blob/master/files/AaronLeonard-CV-July2025.pdf).
